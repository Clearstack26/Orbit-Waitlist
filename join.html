After every conversation

The card goes in your pocket.

By tomorrow, it's all gone, life takes over.

You tell yourself you'll remember. You open your notes and never write it down. By the time you want to reach out, the card has fallen through a crack, gone through the wash, or simply disappeared.

You think about messaging them. But you can't remember their company, their role, or what you actually talked about.

With Orbit, you scan their card or record a quick voice note just after the conversation. No card? No problem. Orbit builds a profile for them, for that event, and personalises a follow-up ready to go when the event is over. Simple, easy, all in one.
